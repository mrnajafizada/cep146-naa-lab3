# Simple Caprese Skewers

A quick, fresh, and delicious appetizer.

## Ingredients
* 1 pint cherry tomatoes
* 8 oz fresh mozzarella pearls
* 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
* 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
* 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
* Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. **Assemble:** Thread a cherry tomato, a folded basil leaf, and a mozzarella pearl onto a small wooden skewer (toothpick).
2. **Arrange:** Place the completed skewers on a serving platter.
3. **Drizzle:** Just before serving, drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
4. **Season:** Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and fresh cracked pepper.
