# Classic Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe yields soft, buttery cookies with crisp edges and melty chocolate pockets.

## Ingredients
* 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1 cup packed brown sugar
* 2 eggs
* 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 2 teaspoons hot water
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 3 cups all-purpose flour
* 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Preheat: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Cream Butter & Sugars: Beat butter and sugars until smooth.
3. Mix Wet Ingredients: Beat in eggs and vanilla.
4. Dissolve Soda: Dissolve baking soda in hot water; add to batter with salt.
5. Combine: Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
6. Bake: Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes.
